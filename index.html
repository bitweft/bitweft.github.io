My Superhero!

I wandered into a lane that I thought would take me home, 
Shocked I was to see a wild tiger there left alone. 

I turned and tried to run 
But that animal wanted to have fun! 

It was hungry and wanted my meat
 I was frightened to think that it would have a wonderful treat. 

I called out to spider man for help 
He came but the tiger got better of him and I could hear only yelp. 

Then I called for super-man 
He didn’t do much, so he lost his good ‘fan’. (that was me) 

Then Batman came dressed in black 
The tiger fought him and sent him back. 

I wanted to wail 
Would anybody win over the tiger and not refuse to fail? 

Then to save me, came my dad 
He felt he could finish the animal that was bad. 

He fought it with all his might 
But I was afraid that the tiger would bite. 

But dad knew that he wouldn’t fall
 Coz his love for me would always stand tall. 

He was right and the tiger was killed,
 And with tremendous amount of joy, I was filled. 

But, before I could hug him, buzzed my phone 
I realized that it was my alarm tone. 

Oh! I sighed. It was only a dream
 Heck, my dad is still the king. 

No matter who the world thinks is the best,
 I know for sure that my dad is above the rest! 

Thank You Pa for being my SUPER HERO!!
