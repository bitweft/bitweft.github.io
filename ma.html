Ma

It has been long since the time you took birth
And there is no word or thing that can explain your worth

In all these years, tough or smooth times you have seen
You overcame all obstacles and strong you have always been

To your parents, you were a wonderful daughter
Your love for them was deeper than the ocean’s water

An awesome sister you are
Your siblings obviously consider you a star

Then you became the most wonderful wife
And Pa cannot imagine without you; a life

Soon later, you became a mother
That’s the relation that can’t be replaced by any other

You brought me to this world, which is known to be big and bad
But you protected me in your shelter of love and always kept me glad

You taught me to talk
You held my hand and made me walk

You sang for me bhajans and nursery rhymes
To me, your voice was sweeter than the wind chimes

Karate, you wanted me to learn
When I didn’t want to go, you became strict and stern

You let me enjoy; you let me play
You allowed me to take decisions and many times let me do things my way

But you always guided me and gave me support
You motivated me to do a lot of things including studying, driving and sport

You gave me love, you gave me your smile
You also taught me a lot of style

You told me about good values and taught me to pray
You explained to me to keep bad things at bay

My frustration, shouting and tantrums you boar
You sat and spoke to me calmly even after tiring work and everyday chore

A million things you have done for me
By appreciating you with words or even my deeds; justice it wont be

All I want to say right now is Thank you for being with me through thick and thin
And I know you will be by my side even in the future as you have in the past always been

Thank you Ma for shaping me and making me who I am.
