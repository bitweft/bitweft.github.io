Farewell to a Friend

We may call this day our ‘farewell’ day
But it’s definitely not an end, I say
It’s just the beginning of the new roads that we take
So that, ‘wonderful’, our lives we make

So many times we have been together
At the bus stop, lectures or to buy wallets made of leather
Picnics are obviously things that we will remember
And ya, you are like my family member.

We have had great times
Have sung together nursery rhymes
Worked together day and night
And fought in the break for a tasty byte

College without you, would have been worse than hell
You made it better, that’s all I can tell.
You were there to bring, with your PJ’s, a lot of cheer
And of course, in joy making us shed a tear.

No matter, we will be how far or near
You will be a friend very dear
Who ignored and forgot all the fights
And who strived hard to set things right

Memorable were these 4 years
And I apologise if I hurt you by holding my ears
All fun times are inscribed in my heart deep
And I know, this, even you will in your heart always keep

All I can say now is each moment spent with you was a great treasure
Beyond any measure!
May you have success always, I pray
And may always on the top you stay.

And thanks to you my dearest friend for those wonderful times and memories!
