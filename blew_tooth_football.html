Blew Tooth Football

This that I speak of is experience true
Incidents like this are definitely few.

It may be funny and to some memorable
But for me it was just a day terrible.

I was up at seven on that fateful Thursday
Because, fell on my face those golden sun rays.

College I was, raring to go
For my football match, against the EXTC foes

I got out of bed and imagined myself on the field
Dreamt about the good results that our teams practice would yield

Kicking the ball towards me, I saw my midfielder team-mate
All I had to do was to head the ball into the goal straight.

I actually ran ahead and leapt high to head the ball
But banged myself against the bedroom wall

Came back to reality coz of the hard impact
And realized that my front tooth was no longer intact

I didn’t want to smile, I didn’t want to laugh
I didn’t want anyone to see my tooth that was half

Didn’t want to go to college that day
But then there was a match that I had to play.

Popped a pain-killer and to college I went
And told all my friends the morning’s incident

They laughed, and photographs of the chipped tooth they wanted to click
And even I was amused thinking I was such a foolish football freak
